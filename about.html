About Jay Egger

Who I Am

I'm Jay Egger — a marketer who codes. I've built my career at the intersection of creative strategy and technical execution, because I believe the best marketing isn't just planned — it's built.

With experience spanning digital marketing, growth strategy, and software development, I bring a unique perspective to every project. I'm as comfortable writing a campaign brief as I am writing a Python script. I think in funnels and functions, headlines and HTTP requests.

I'm driven by the idea that marketers who understand technology — and technologists who understand marketing — are the ones who build things that actually work. That's the space I operate in.

My Approach

I think the best work happens when you can see the full picture. Too many marketers treat technology as a black box, and too many engineers treat marketing as an afterthought. I refuse to do either.

Every project I take on — whether it's a growth campaign, a web app, or an automation pipeline — I approach with the same mindset: understand the user, define the problem, build the solution, measure the results.
